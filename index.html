The Simon Game

  1. Press the play button to begin.
  2. One square will flash and the game has begun.
  3. After you have pressed it, another will flash. Maybe the same one, maybe a different one.
  4. Now, press the first again and follow up with the one that flashed afterwards.
  5. Keep adding to the sequence!
  6. If you press the wrong square in order, the game is over. But you can begin again by clicking Start Again.
